Simon Says

I decided to recreate the classic 90's toy, Simon Says! How the game works is that Simon will glow with a random color, and then it's your turn to click on the same color. Simon will then repeat that color, along with another random color, and it's up to you to continue remembering the long pattern of colors every turn, until you make an error. There is no true winner, only a score for every turn you've succeeded.

  • Client: General Assembly WDI
  • Date: February 2017
  • Techs: HTML5, CSS3, jQuery
